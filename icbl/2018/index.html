International Conference on Blended Learning

Osaka, Japan | 31 Jul - 2 Aug 2018

You are cordially invited to participate in the International Conference on Blended Learning (ICBL 2018) to be held at Kansai University, Osaka, Japan on 31 Jul to 2 Aug 2018.

Undoubtedly, blended learning has become one of the promising approaches to teaching and learning. It aims to integrate traditional learning with innovative means, such as e-learning and open online learning, in order to create a new learning environment to enhance learning effectiveness and enrich learning experience. ICBL2018 provides a platform for knowledge exchange and experience sharing among researchers and practitioners in this field.

The conference theme of ICBL 2018 is Blended Learning : Enhancing Learning Success.

The ICBL 2018 Springer's Best/Excellent Paper Awards will be granted to authors of papers with exceptional merits. The best and excellent papers will be recommended to Journal of Computing in Higher Education (SSCI listed, impact factor 1.440) in a Special Issue on Blended Learning Practices for Enhancing Learning Success. Papers in the area of learning analytics with exceptional merits will be recommended to Journal of Computers in Education (ESCI listed) in a Special Issue on Trends in Learning Analytics. Papers in the area of innovative learning with exceptional merits will be recommended to International Journal on Innovation and Learning (ESCI listed) in a Special Issue on Enhancing Learning Effectiveness with Innovative Multifaceted Learning.

The ICBL 2018 conference proceedings has been published by Springer as Lecture Notes in Computer Science, Vol. 10949. Online access is available here (https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-319-94505-7).
