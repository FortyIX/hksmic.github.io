Early-Bird Rate
(Register on or before 30 April 2018)		Regular Rate

* The amounts in US dollars are for reference ONLY. Please note the exchange rate may fluctuate.

Notes:
  • Every paper must have at least one Full Registration.
  • Full Registration includes admission to all conference sessions, the conference proceedings, coffee breaks, lunches, dinners and the conference banquet.
  • "Participation Only" Registration includes admission to all conference sessions, coffee breaks, lunches, dinners and the banquet. No conference proceedings is provided.
  • Extra conference proceedings can be purchased at HKD 500 per copy.
