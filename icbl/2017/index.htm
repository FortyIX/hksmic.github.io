You are cordially invited to participate in the International Conference on Blended Learning (ICBL2017) to be held at City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong SAR on 27 to 29 June 2017.

Undoubtedly, blended learning has become one of the promising approaches to teaching and learning. It aims to integrate traditional learning with innovative means, such as e-learning and open online learning, in order to create a new learning environment to enhance learning effectiveness and enrich learning experience. ICBL2017 provides a platform for knowledge exchange and experience sharing among researchers and practitioners in this field.

The conference theme of ICBL2017 is Blended Learning : New Challenges and Innovative Practices.
