Call for Papers

You are cordially invited to participate in the 9th International Conference on Blended Learning (ICBL 2016) to be held at Peking University in Beijing, China on 19 to 21 July 2016.

Undoubtedly, blended learning has become one of the promising approaches to teaching and learning. It aims to integrate traditional learning with innovative means, such as e-learning and open online learning, in order to create a new learning environment to enhance learning effectiveness and enrich learning experience. ICBL 2016 provides a platform for knowledge exchange and experience sharing among researchers and practitioners in this field.

The conference theme of ICBL 2016 is Blended Learning : Aligning Theory with Practice. Original papers reporting research results or novel applications of blended learning are welcomed. ICBL2016 covers all topics in blended learning including, but not limited to:

  • Adaptive Learning
  • Computer Supported Collaborative Learning
  • Content Development for Blended Learning
  • Experiences in Blended Learning
  • Improved Flexibility of Learning Processes
  • Institutional Policies for Blended Learning
  • Interactive Blended Learning Solutions
  • Open Education Resources
  • Organizational Frameworks for Blended Learning
  • Pedagogical and Psychological Issues
  • Practices in Borderless Education
  • Strategies for Blended Learning

Paper Submission

Papers should be written in English and submitted through the conference website. The Conference Proceedings will be published by Springer under the Lecture Notes in Computer Science Series, and submitted to ISI CPCI, ACM Digital Library and EI indexing. Selected papers will be recommended to the International Journal of Innovation and Learning. Best and excellent paper awards will be granted to authors of papers with exceptional merits.

Please submit your paper through the submission website. Your paper should follow the Springer’s LNCS format in 10 to 12 pages.

Submission Deadline : 15 March 2016
