Venue: City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong SAR

The ISET 2017 conference will take place in City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon Tong, Kowloon, Hong Kong SAR.


Getting to City University of Hong Kong:
By Mass Transit Railway (MTR):
  • Get off at Kowloon Tong station. (MTR Journey Planner)
  • Find Exit C to Festival Walk then take Exit C2.
  • In Festival Walk, go up to Level LG1.
  • Find Shop LG1-10, take the escalator next to it, which brings you to a pedestrian subway leading to CityU.
  • After walking through the pedestrian subway, go down the staircase on your right and follow the directional signs, you will find yourself walking under a covered corridor alongside the garden which will lead you to the University Circle.
  • Go along the covered walkway and follow the directional signs which will lead you to Academic 2 (AC2), Academic 3 (AC3) or Cheng Yick-chi Building depending on the presentation venue.

    • By taxi:
  • Ask the taxi driver to take you to City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon Tong, Kowloon and drop you off at the University Circle. (Address label)
  • Go along the covered walkway and follow the directional signs which will lead you to Academic 2 (AC2), Academic 3 (AC3) or Cheng Yick-chi Building depending on the presentation venue.
