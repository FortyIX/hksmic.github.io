Call for Papers

You are cordially invited to participate in the 3rd International Symposium on Educational Technology (ISET2017) to be held at City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China on 27 to 29 June 2017.

Technology has become an irreversible force driving changes in teaching and learning practices. Educational technology broadly covers instructional technology, information and communication technology for educational purposes, with an aim to enhance learning outcome and enrich learning experience through the effective and innovative use of technology. ISET2017 provides a platform for knowledge exchange and experience sharing among researchers and practitioners in this field.

Original papers reporting research results or novel applications of educational technology are welcomed. ISET2017 covers all topics in educational technology including, but not limited to:

  • e-learning and online learning
  • instructional technology
  • ubiquitous learning and flexible learning
  • social media for teaching and learning
  • massive open online courses
  • open education resources
  • online platform and environment
  • podcasting and broadcasting
  • virtual classroom
  • virtual reality for teaching and learning
  • learning analytics
  • institutional policies and strategies

Paper Submission

Original papers should be written in English and submitted through the conference website. Your paper should follow the IEEE's two-column proceedings format in 4 to 5 pages.

The Conference Proceedings will be published by the IEEE Computer Society CPS and submitted to the IEEE Xplore, IEEE Computer Society Digital Library and EI indexing. Selected papers will be recommended to the International Journal of Innovation and Learning (Inderscience). Best and excellent paper awards will be granted to authors of papers with exceptional merits.

Please submit your paper through the submission website. Your paper should follow the IEEE’s two-column format in 4 to 5 pages (regular papers) or 2 to 3 pages (short papers).

Submission Deadline : 28 February 2017
