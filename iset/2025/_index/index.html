11th International Symposium on Educational Technology

22 – 25 July 2025 | Bangkok, Thailand

You are cordially invited to participate in the 11th International Symposium on Educational Technology (ISET 2025) to be held in Bangkok, Thailand on 22 to 25 July 2025.

Broadly covering instructional technology, information and communication technology for educational purposes, educational technology aims to enhance learning flexibility, improve learning effectiveness and enrich learning experience. It has become an irreversible force driving changes in teaching and learning practices, not only on the content delivery but also the learning process, in order to make learning become more sustainable, flexible and smart. ISET 2025 aims to provide a platform for knowledge exchange and experience sharing among researchers and practitioners in the field.

The symposium theme is Technological Innovation : Sustainable and Flexible Smart Learning. Original papers reporting the latest research findings and novel applications of technological innovation for teaching and learning are welcome. ISET 2025 covers topics related to educational technology including, but not limited to: • Application of AI in Learning • AR, VR and MR in Learning • E-learning and online learning • Game-based learning • Improved Flexibility of Learning Process • Institutional policies and strategies • Instructional technology • Intelligent Tutoring Systems • Learning analytics and education big data • Massive open online courses • Mobile Learning and Ubiquitous Learning • Online platform and environment • Open education resources • Personalized learning • Podcasting and broadcasting • Practices in Borderless Education • Smart Learning Environment • Social media for teaching and learning
