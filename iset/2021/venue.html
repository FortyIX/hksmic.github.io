Transportation

  • From Nagoya (Meitetsu Nagoya) station: Go to Otagawa station by Meitetsu Tokoname/Kowa/New Chita Line (17 min. by express).
  • From Central Japan International Airport Nagoya (Chubu Centrair): Go to Otagawa station by Meitetsu Tokoname Line (19 min. by express).
  • From the south exit, you can see the University on the right side, just 5 minutes distance on foot.
