10th International Symposium on Educational Technology

29 July to 1 August 2024 | Macao SAR, China

Conference Venue

The conference venue of the 10th International Symposium on Educational Technology (ISET 2024) is the Galaxy International Convention Center in Andaz Macau (Google Map, Baidu Map).

The conference events will be held in room 23, 24, 25, and 26 on the 2/F of the Galaxy International Convention Center.

The lunch gatherings and conference banquet will be held in Hall B on the G/F of the Galaxy International Convention Center.
